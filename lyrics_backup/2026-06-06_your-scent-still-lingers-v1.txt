[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
I can feel your shadow on the empty side of the bed
Tangled in the cotton where you laid your weary head
I trace the empty spaces where we used to intertwine
Bathing in the memory, I pretend that you're still mine

[Verse 1]
The midnight rain is drumming on the windowpane
A solitary rhythm driving me insane
A faded denim jacket resting on the chair
I pull it close to me to find you hiding there

[Pre-Chorus]
There's a phantom warmth still floating in the room
A ghostly little trace of your sweet perfume
My fingers crave the feeling of your skin so near
I close my tired eyes and softly pull you here

[Chorus]
Your scent still lingers on my favorite shirt
A quiet kind of magic mixed with gentle hurt
It wraps around my senses like a steady flame
And leaves my aching heart whispering your name

[Post-Chorus]
It lingers on when the daylight fades away
It lingers on and begs for you to stay

[Verse 2]
We danced inside the kitchen by the firelight
A pair of reckless shadows tearing through the night
I poured a heavy glass and watched you slowly sway
A beautiful collision I could never wash away

[Pre-Chorus 2]

[Chorus]

[Post-Chorus 2]

[Bridge]
The hours slowly drag across the wooden floor
I find myself just staring at the open door
The silence is a river pulling me apart
A heavy rhythm beating deep inside the heart
Just Saeed confessing every single midnight sin
Where the loneliness ends and the longing begins

[Spoken]
[Whispered] (I just need to breathe you in one more time)
[Intimate] (Because this empty room is so cold without you)
